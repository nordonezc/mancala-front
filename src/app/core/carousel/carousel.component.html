Players Turn

The player who begins picks up all the stones in any of his own six pits, and sows the stones on to the right, one in each of the following pits, including his own big pit. No stones are put in the opponents' big pit.

Repeat turn

If the player's last stone lands in his own big pit, he gets another turn. This can be repeated several times before it's the other player's turn.

Capturing Stones

Always when the last stone lands in an own empty pit, the player captures his own stone and all stones in the opposite pit and puts them in his own mancala.

The Game Ends

The game is over as soon as one of the sides runs out of stones. The player who still has stones in his pits keeps them and puts them in his big pit.

Winner

The winner of game is the player who has the most stones in his big pit.
